Responsibility and Accountability

Our company takes responsibility for the impact of our technology on society and the environment. We will ensure that our operations comply with all environmental laws and regulations, and we will take responsibility for any harm caused by our technology. We will also be transparent and accountable in our regulation and correction processes. Finally, we will acknowledge our mistakes and be open about our plans to correct them, what the mistake was, and how we can inform the future with our past mistakes.
