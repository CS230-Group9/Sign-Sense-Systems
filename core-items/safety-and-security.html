Safety and Security

Our company understands that there are inherent challenges in working with personal communication, and we strive to prioritize the data security of our customers, employees, and the general public in everything we do. We are constantly scrutinizing our technology to ensure accuracy and avoid bias in our products. We also focus heavily on the data security and safety of our customers, and we refuse to sell data to companies with detectable malicious intent. Finally, we will ensure that our technology is ethically sourced, and created in an environment that treats everyone fairly and equally.
