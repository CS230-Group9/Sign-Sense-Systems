Priorities

1. Accuracy

The highest priority experiment to test first is Experiment 1, focusing on the accuracy and efficiency of our computer vision translation technology. The success of our product hinges on its ability to accurately and efficiently translate sign language.

2. Accessibility and Affordability

The second priority experiment to test would be Experiment 3, focusing on the accessibility and affordability of our technology. It is essential to ensure that our product is user-friendly and financially accessible to a wide range of potential users. By refining our user interface and pricing model based on user feedback, we can create a product that effectively addresses the needs of our target market.

3. Effectiveness of Strategic Partnerships and Collaborations

The third priority experiment to test would be Experiment 2, focusing on the effectiveness of strategic partnerships and collaborations. This experiment will help us identify the most promising partnership opportunities and refine our approach to forming long-lasting, mutually beneficial collaborations.
