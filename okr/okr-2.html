Accessibility and Affordability

For this experiment, we can create a mock-up of how our technology would integrate into a potential partner's platform or service. Using design tools like Adobe XD or Figma, we can create a visual representation of the integration without actually developing a functional prototype.

We can then conduct surveys and interviews with potential partners and customers to assess their interest in and perceived benefits of the integration. This feedback will help us refine our partnership proposals and better understand the value proposition for potential partners.

Measurable outcomes:

1. The percentage of positive responses from potential partners.

2. The number of partnerships established within a given timeframe.
